ABOUT THE PROJECT

Luma21 is a new project by TownX Development

TownX a boutique developer that is based in Dubai and has already delivered many residential projects across the emirate including Easy18, Easy19 and East40.

Luma21, launched in Q3 2021, is a 5-storey residential development that offers four types of apartments ideal for both professionals and families with children. Potential buyers and investors can choose the unit of their dreams from a collection of studios and 1-3 bedroom apartments with living areas ranging from 489 sq.ft to 1,264 sq.ft. All units are fairly spacious and are provided with all the necessary amenities. Each unit has a balcony where one can relax after a tiring day, while each bedroom offers an en-suite bathroom in 2-3 bedroom apartments.

Lots of recreational options

Residents can enjoy gorgeous views of the Dubai skyline from the communal rooftop swimming pool, and those who are into sports will appreciate the on-site gym with state-of-the-art fitness equipment, with a seperate area for men and women.

Floor-to-ceiling panoramic windows

Guaranteeing maximum natural light and amazing views of the surrounding luscious gardens from the bedroom

Security

Concierge services, as well as security will be available to all residents

Obtaining an apartment in Luma21 is the perfect option for those who wish to experience a quiet lifestyle in Dubai, but whilst also having the opportunity to get to any corner of the emirate in several minutes.

LOCATION

LUMA21 IS LOCATED IN JUMEIRAH VILLAGE CIRCLE (JVC) AND HAS EASY ACCESS TO THE E44 ROAD (AL KHAIL ROAD, RAS AL KHOR ROAD, AL AWEER ROAD, DUBAI-HATTA HIGHWAY).

Residents of the development can easily get to any corner of Dubai, which means it will take about 30 minutes to get from Luma21 to Business Bay, Downtown Dubai, Dubai Marina and Dubai International Airport.

Jumeirah Village Circle is one of the most family-friendly communities in the city, and as a result, residents can easily find a wide range of educational institutions here: Kids World Nursery JVC and Little Wonders Nursery JVC are less than a 10-minute drive. JSS International School which follows the curriculum by the Indian Certificate of Secondary Education (ICSE), Sunmarke School with a British curriculum, and Dubai Schools Al Barsha offering an American curriculum, are less than a 20-minute drive from Luma21.

In terms of healthcare, Right Health Karama Medical Center, offering general medical services, and Apex Dental & Orthodontic Clinic JVC are the closest options.

It takes 10-minutes to get to both destinations from the residential development.

As for hospitals, Emirates Hospital Day Surgery & Medical Center in Motor City is a 20-minute drive.

Residents of Luma21 can easily reach several shopping malls. Thus, a trip to Circle Mall will take only 10 minutes. Here one can find 80 retail stores, 40 F&B options, a 60,000 sq.ft gym space, a swimming pool with programmes for children and adults, as well as much more. The more famous shopping destinations of Mall of the Emirates and The Dubai Mall can be reached in a 30-minute drive from Luma21.

Among the nearest beaches that are located within a 30-minute drive of the development areKite Beach and JBR Beach. It takes just 15 minutes to get from the residential development to Dubai Miracle Garden, which is one of the most popular attractions in Dubai. It’s the largest flower garden in the world where one can see over 50 million flowers. Dubai Butterfly Garden, which houses more than 50,000 butterflies from 50 different categories, can be reached within the same distance.

PROPERTY PRICES

Due to the fact that Luma21 is located in freehold property zone both locals and foreigners have the right to obtain a unit in this unique residential building. The price of a 1-bedroom apartment with an area of 700 sq.ft starts from AED 595,000 (USD 162,000). There is also an opportunity to avail a payment plan from the developer, with a 10% down payment and then pay 50% of the purchase price after the building is completed.
